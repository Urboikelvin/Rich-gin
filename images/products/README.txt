TO REPLACE A PHOTO:
Save your new photo over the existing file using the SAME name
(e.g. gloves.jpg), or point that product's <img src="..."> in index.html
at a new file name. Roughly 900x620px, product centered, JPG or PNG,
works best.

TO ADD A BRAND-NEW PRODUCT CARD:
In index.html, find the PRODUCTS section, copy one whole <div class="pchip">
block, give it a new image file and label, and paste it into the grid.

Note: the Products section also has a banner at the bottom telling visitors
this isn't the full catalog and to contact you for anything not shown —
so you don't need a card for every single product you stock.
